FAMOUS PLACES

KRABIS RAILAY BEACH

About Krabis Railay Beach:

Krabi Beach, a stunning destination in southern Thailand!
1. Location: Krabi Province, southern Thailand
2. Beaches: Railay, Ao Nang, Klong Muang, Tubkaak
3. Islands: Phi Phi Islands, Koh Lanta, Koh Poda
4. Population: Approximately 30,000
5. Language: Thai (English widely spoken)

Best Season TO visit

1. High Season (December to March): Cool temperatures (25-30°C/77-86°F), calm seas.
2. Shoulder Season (April to June, September to November): Warm temperatures (28-32°C/82-90°F), fewer tourists.
3. Avoid Low Season (July to August): Rainy, rough seas.

KOH SAMUI ISLAND

About Koh Samui Island :

Koh Samui Island, a tropical paradise in the Gulf of Thailand!
1. Location: Surat Thani Province, southern Thailand
2. Size: Approximately 25 km (15.5 miles) in diameter
3. Population: Around 50,000
4. Language: Thai (English widely spoken)
5. Currency: Thai Baht (THB)

Best Season to Visit:

1. High Season (December to March): Cool temperatures (25-30°C/77-86°F), calm seas.
2. Shoulder Season (April to June, September to November): Warm temperatures (28-32°C/82-90°F), fewer tourists.
3. Avoid Low Season (July to August): Rainy, rough seas.

CHIANG MAIS OLD CITY

About Chiang Mais Old City

Chiang Mai's Old City, a cultural and spiritual hub in northern Thailand!
1. Location: Chiang Mai Province, northern Thailand
2. Size: Approximately 2.5 km² (1 sq mi)
3. Population: Around 200,000
4. Language: Thai (English widely spoken)
5. Currency: Thai Baht (THB)

Best Season to Visit:

1. Cool Season (November to February): Comfortable temperatures (15-25°C/59-77°F).
2. Shoulder Season (March to May, September to October): Warm temperatures (20-30°C/68-86°F).
3. Avoid Hot Season (June to August): Temperatures up to 40°C (104°F).
