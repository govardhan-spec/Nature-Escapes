Milans duomo

About Milans Duomo

1. Location: Piazza del Duomo, Milan, Italy
2. Date: Construction began in 1386, completed in 1965
3. Style: Gothic architecture
4. Size: 157 meters (515 feet) long, 109 meters (357 feet) wide, 108 meters (354 feet) high
5. Materials: Marble, granite, and Candoglia stone

Festivals and Events:

1. Milan Fashion Week (February and September)
2. Milan Design Week (April)
3. Festa della Repubblica (June 2nd)
4. Festa di Sant'Ambrogio (December 7th)
5. Christmas Markets (December)

AMALFIS COAST

About Amalfis coast

1. Location: Southern Italy, Campania region
2. Length: Approximately 50 km (31 miles) long
3. Towns: Positano, Amalfi, Ravello, Sorrento, Pompeii
4. Features: Cliffside towns, crystal-clear waters, stunning scenery
5. UNESCO World Heritage Site: 1997

Festivals and Events:

1. Amalfi Coast Music Festival (July-August)
2. Ravello Festival (July-September)
3. Positano Film Festival (July)
4. Sorrento Carnival (February-March)
5. Christmas Markets (December)

LAKE COMO

About Lake Como

1. Location: Lombardy, Italy
2. Size: Approximately 56 km (35 miles) long, 4 km (2.5 miles) wide
3. Depth: Maximum depth of 410 meters (1,345 feet)
4. Altitude: 199 meters (653 feet) above sea level
5. Surrounding towns: Bellagio, Varenna, Menaggio, Como

Festivals and Events:

1. Festa della Repubblica (June 2nd)
2. Festa di San Giovanni (June 24th)
3. Lake Como Music Festival (July-August)
4. Bellagio Festival (September)
5. Christmas Markets (December)
