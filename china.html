FAMOUS PLACES

GREAT WALL OF CHINA

About Great Wall of China:

The Great Wall of China, one of the world's most impressive architectural achievements!
1. Location: Northern China, across multiple provinces
2. Length: Approximately 13,171 miles (21,196 km)
3. Built: 7th century BC to 16th century AD
4. Purpose: Military defense, border control, and symbolic representation
5. UNESCO World Heritage Site: 1987

Best Season to Visit:

Best Season to Visit: 1. Spring (April to May): Mild temperatures (15-25°C/59-77°F), fewer crowds
2. Autumn (September to October): Comfortable temperatures (10-20°C/50-68°F), scenic foliage
3. Shoulder Season (March to April, November to December): Fewer tourists, lower prices

TERRACOTTA WARRIORS

About Terracotta Warriors:

The Terracotta Warriors, one of China's most fascinating archaeological wonders!
1. Location: Xi'an, Shaanxi Province, China
2. Discovered: 1974
3. Date: Built around 210 BC (Qin Dynasty)
4. Purpose: Life-sized army for the first emperor's afterlife
5. UNESCO World Heritage Site: 1987

Best Season to Visit:

1. Spring (April to May): Mild temperatures (15-25°C/59-77°F), fewer crowds
2. Autumn (September to October): Comfortable temperatures (10-20°C/50-68°F), scenic foliage
3. Shoulder Season (March to April, November to December): Fewer tourists, lower prices

YELLOW MOUNTAINS

About Yellow MOUNTAINS:

The Yellow Mountains (Huangshan), a breathtaking UNESCO World Heritage Site in China!
1. Location: Anhui Province, Eastern China
2. Name: Huangshan (Yellow Mountains)
3. Date: Formed over 100 million years ago
4. Features: Granite peaks, pine forests, and scenic valleys
5. UNESCO World Heritage Site: 1990

Best Season to Visit:

1. Spring (April to May): Mild temperatures (15-25°C/59-77°F), blooming flowers
2. Autumn (September to October): Comfortable temperatures (10-20°C/50-68°F), scenic foliage
3. Shoulder Season (March to April, November to December): Fewer tourists, lower prices

About River Guilin

The Li River, a breathtaking scenic destination in Guilin, China!
1. Location: Guilin, Guangxi Province, Southern China
2. Length: Approximately 426 km (265 miles)
3. Origin: Mao'er Mountains
4. Features: Karst landscape, lush vegetation, and picturesque villages
5. UNESCO World Heritage Site: 2013

Best Season to Visit:

FORBBIDEN CITY

About Forbbiden City

The Forbidden City, a majestic imperial palace complex in Beijing, China!
1. Location: Beijing, China
2. Date: Built in 1406-1420 (Ming Dynasty)
3. Size: 7,800,000 square feet (180 acres)
4. Function: Imperial palace for 24 emperors (Ming and Qing Dynasties)
5. UNESCO World Heritage Site: 1987

Best Season to Visit

1. Spring (April to May): Mild temperatures (15-25°C/59-77°F), fewer crowds
2. Autumn (September to October): Comfortable temperatures (10-20°C/50-68°F), scenic foliage
3. Shoulder Season (March to April, November to December): Fewer tourists, lower prices
